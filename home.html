Who We Are

The Weare Pond Hockey League was founded under two very important principles: competition and fun. It was founded by a small group of friends who are seeking other teams for some old fashion pond hockey. Any level of talent is welcome; our leagueincludes anywhere from absolute beginners to fairly skilled hockey players. So if you're a bender, don't feel intimidated and come play some free and easy hockey.



What's Happening

Currently, the Gravy Train leads the Pond Dogs 2 Boots to 1. A Boot, in WPHL terms, is a championship win. At the end of a series, the winning team get's to hoist a boot, typically L.L. Bean, which is equivolent to the Stanley Cup.
